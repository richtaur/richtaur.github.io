Game Portfolio

I have developed over a dozen HTML5 games published across Steam, Google Play, iOS App Store, including:

A Wizard's Lizard

An overhead action-RPG roguelike that continues on after death. Released on Steam in 2014, it has sold more than 50,000 copies in over 10 countries.

  • Design: Matt Hackett and Geoff Blair
  • Programming: Matt Hackett and Geoff Blair
  • Art: Matt Hackett
  • Music: Joshua Morse

Indie Game Sim

A simulation about becoming an indie game developer, released on Steam in 2016. Create games, share them with friends, and defeat your inner demons.

  • Design: Matt Hackett
  • Programming: Matt Hackett
  • Art: Matt Hackett
  • Music: Joshua Morse

A Wizard's Lizard: Soul Thief

Play as the monsters in this sequel with improved graphics and more harmonious mechanics. Released on Steam Early Access in 2016.

  • Design: Matt Hackett and Geoff Blair
  • Programming: Matt Hackett and Geoff Blair
  • Art: Matt Hackett
  • Music: Joshua Morse
